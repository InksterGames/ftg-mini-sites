How It Works

How do you play Never Have I Ever?

One person reads a statement aloud. Anyone who HAS done that thing takes a drink or loses a point. The FTG edition adds Australian-chaos flavor and three intensity levels.

How many statements are there?

45+ unique statements across three modes. The generator tracks what is been shown and resets automatically when the pool runs dry.

What is Chug Mode?

Toggle Chug Mode on and anyone who is done the thing must drink. Perfect for pregaming.

Is it free?

Completely free. No signup, no ads. If you want the full chaos experience, grab the FTG card deck.

Can I play this virtually?

Absolutely. Share the link in your group chat or on a video call. Everyone can see the statements on their own phone.
