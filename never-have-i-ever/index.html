How It Works

How do you play Never Have I Ever?

One person reads a statement. Anyone who HAS done that thing takes a drink or loses a point. The FTG edition adds Australian-chaos flavor and three intensity levels.

How many statements are there?

45+ unique statements across three modes (15 Mild, 15 Spicy, 15 Wild). The generator tracks what's been shown and resets automatically — no repeats until you've seen them all.

What's Chug Mode?

Toggle Chug Mode on and anyone who's done the thing must drink. Perfect for pregaming or when the vibe calls for liquid courage.

Can I play this virtually?

Absolutely. Share the tool link in your group chat or on a video call. The share button makes it dead easy to spread the chaos.

Is it free?

Completely free. No signup, no ads. Just pure chaotic party energy. Want the full chaos experience? Grab the FTG card deck at f--kthegame.com.
